This is the first post on my blog. I plan to write here about whatever I happen to be building or thinking about — data engineering, side projects, tools I like, things I've learned the hard way.

## Why a blog

I find that writing forces clarity. If I can't explain something, I don't actually understand it. So this is as much a learning tool for me as anything else.

## What to expect

No schedule, no theme, no newsletter. Just occasional posts when I have something worth putting down.
